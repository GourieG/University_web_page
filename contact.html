About Us

Toc H Institute of Science & Technology was established in 2002.
It is located in Arakkunnam, Ernakulam, Kerala, India.
The campus has three blocks: The Main Block named after the Indian Scientist Aryabhata Einstein Block Visweshwarayya Block.
