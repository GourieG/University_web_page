Our Facilities

Our Library

The TIST Library supports the education mission of the college by discerning and fulfilling the information needs of its diverse user community. The main library, located centrally, facilitates easy access to all user communities, along with MBA and Digital libraries and 10 other department reference libraries. The 10000 square feet library provides a wide range of services. The full-fledged library with its automated library system is fully equipped to meet any requirements of all information seekers. The library services to the students and faculty are made available through Vivekananda Central, Sarabhai Digital & C K Prahalad MBA Libraries.

Our Play Ground

A multi-purpose play ground on the campus to conduct Out- door games such as Soccer, Basketball, Volleyball, Tennis, Cricket ,Track & Field activities and In-door games like Table Tennis, Badminton, Chess and Carom. Intramural and extramural activities are planned to ensure regular participation of the students in game and sports throughout the year including Inter-Collegiate tournaments of CUSAT & KTU.

Transportation

19 Buses, 01 Water Bowser, 01 Amublance, 02 Cars and 04 Light Duty Vehicles including 02 Motor cycle are catering the requirements of students/staff and other activities of the college. Bus route covering most of the areas in Ernakulam (Eramalloor, Aroor, Fortkochi, Edacochi, Njarackal, Kalloor Kalamassery, Aluva, Eloor, Kolencherry & Muvattupuzha,) and Kottayam (Kaduthuruthy, Kurupumthara, Thalayolaparambu & Vaikom) districts. Total of 1069 students/staff are availing college bus facility.

Hostel

Separate safe and secure in-campus hostels are provided for boys and girls with capacity to accommodate 700 inmates with well-furnished, comfortable rooms in a pleasant and hygienic environment. There are separate buildings meant both for the graduate and post-graduate students as well. Vegetarian and non-vegetarian meals are cooked and served separately as per requirement. Common facilities like mess, study rooms, TV, recreation, internet (Wi-Fi) etc. are arranged and run in the best possible way, to provide a good and amicable atmosphere for the constructive mental development of the students.

Our Labs

Augmented with a modern computer centre, the department offers the best and updated practical training. It is well equipped with 5 diversified laboratories installed with a variety of software catering to the needs of the students and covering the curriculum specified by the affiliated Kerala Technological University. All the computers in the campus, including those in the hostels and all departments, are connected by means of a network so that the students from the hostel and the staff and students from different departments can have direct access to the facilities in the Computer labs round the clock.

Workshops

The workshops are fully equipped with complete set of latest equipments/machines, tools and instruments, which caters to the need of the undergraduate and post graduate students. All laboratories have experienced technical staff from industry. The various labs and workshops encompass the vital aspects of Mechanical Engineering
